⚽ WorldCup · Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Pi Network · worldcup.pi

1. Introduction

WorldCup ("the App") is a community football fan app built on Pi Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the following information through Pi Network authentication:

• Your Pi Network username (provided by Pi SDK upon login)
• Match predictions and points you earn within the app
• Comments you post in the Fan Zone
• Payment records for Premium access (0.5 Pi)

3. How We Use Your Information

• To display your username on the leaderboard
• To track your prediction points and streak
• To process Pi payments for Premium access
• To show your comments in the Fan Zone

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not operate external databases or servers that store your personal information. Pi payment records are processed through Pi Network's secure infrastructure.

5. Pi Network

This app uses Pi Network's SDK for authentication and payments. Your use of Pi Network is governed by Pi Network's own Privacy Policy available at minepi.com.

6. Third Party Data

Match fixture data is sourced from OpenFootball (public domain CC0). No personal data is shared with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact

This app is a community project on Pi Network. For questions, contact us through the Pi Network community.
